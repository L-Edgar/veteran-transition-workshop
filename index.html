Veteran Transition Workshop

The Veteran Transition Workshop is a hands-on, supportive event designed to empower U.S. military veterans as they navigate the shift from active service to civilian life. This workshop offers practical guidance on translating military experience into civilian job qualifications, building strong resumes, preparing for interviews, and accessing vital mental health and professional development resources. Whether you're seeking employment, exploring new skills, or simply looking for direction, this workshop equips you with the tools and confidence to take your next step. Join us and begin your journey toward a successful and fulfilling future beyond the uniform.
